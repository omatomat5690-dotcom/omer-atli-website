About

I am a GMC UK-registered doctor (GMC No. 8126471) and a physician registered with the Turkish Ministry of Health, providing online consultations for patients in the UK and internationally.

This is a private telemedicine service and does not replace your NHS GP. Where appropriate, you will be advised to seek in-person care or contact your GP.

My background is in Emergency Medicine and Primary Care, where I have worked with a wide range of acute and everyday medical problems. These are settings where decisions need to be made quickly, clearly, and safely, and where recognising serious illness early is critical.

I moved into telemedicine after repeatedly seeing patients wait too long for care that could have been assessed much earlier. Many problems do not need to wait days — they need proper assessment, clear advice, and a plan. My role is to provide that: structured, UK-standard medical care, and clear guidance on what to do next.

My consultations follow NICE-aligned, evidence-based practice, with a strong focus on identifying risk and not missing important conditions. I assess carefully, explain clearly, and refer on for in-person review or investigation whenever that is the safer path. You will receive clear explanations, practical advice, and prescriptions where appropriate.

If your condition requires in-person care, urgent review, or specialist input, I will tell you directly.

Consultations are available in English and Turkish.

I provide assessment, advice, and short-term management. Ongoing care and monitoring should be coordinated with your regular GP where applicable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the consultation work?
Consultations are conducted as a video call via WhatsApp - just message to book and Dr Atli will call you directly. If you prefer, Zoom or Google Meet are also available on request.
How quickly will I be seen?
Dr Atli aims to respond promptly and consultations are arranged as soon as practical.
Can I get a prescription online?
Yes. Where clinically appropriate, prescriptions can be issued promptly following your consultation.
What conditions can be treated online?
Dr Atli can assess and treat a wide range of conditions including respiratory, gastrointestinal, urinary, dermatological, sexual health, and weight management concerns. If your situation requires in-person care or emergency attention, you will be advised accordingly.
Is this service confidential?
Absolutely. All consultations are private and your information is handled in accordance with UK GDPR and medical confidentiality standards.
What if I'm travelling or based outside the UK?
Remote consultations are available wherever you are. Dr Atli has experience supporting patients while travelling, including managing medication supply issues abroad.
What do I need for a video consultation?
Just a smartphone or computer with a working camera and microphone. Your consultation will take place via WhatsApp video call - or Zoom or Google Meet if you prefer.

Privacy & Data Protection

Your privacy matters. All personal and medical information shared during consultations is treated as strictly confidential and handled in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing medical care and will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, unless required by law or to protect your safety. Medical records are stored securely and retained in line with NHS record-keeping guidelines.

By booking a consultation, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal and health data for clinical purposes. You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time by contacting dr@omeratli.com.

Medical Disclaimer

This service does not replace emergency care. If you are experiencing severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, signs of stroke, heavy bleeding, or any life-threatening symptoms, call 999 (UK) or 112 (Turkey) immediately. Telemedicine has limitations — a face-to-face assessment may be required for certain conditions.

This service provides private medical consultations and does not replace ongoing NHS primary care. Patients are encouraged to keep their NHS GP informed of any private treatment received.
